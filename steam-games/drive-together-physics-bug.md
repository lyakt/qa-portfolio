# Car spins uncontrollably after landing on spring

Game: Drive Together (Demo)  
Platform: PC (Windows)  
Type: Physics / Gameplay bug  
Reproducibility: Sometimes  

## Steps to reproduce
1. Launch Drive Together Demo
2. Start driving
3. Drive onto a spring object
4. Observe the car behavior after landing

## Expected result
The car should land smoothly and remain stable.

## Actual result
The car bounces and starts spinning very strongly before landing.

## Notes
The issue appears to be related to unstable physics behavior.
